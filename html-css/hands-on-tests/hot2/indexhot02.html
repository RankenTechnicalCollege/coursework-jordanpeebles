About Applebee's

Applebee's Neighborhood Grill & Bar is a popular American restaurant chain known for its casual dining experience and delicious menu options. From classic burgers and steaks to fresh salads and appetizers, Applebee's offers something for everyone.

Founded in 1980, Applebee's has grown to hundreds of locations across the United States and beyond. Whether you're gathering with friends, enjoying a family meal, or just grabbing a quick bite, Applebee's welcomes you with friendly service and a warm atmosphere.

Check out our menu for the latest specials and visit your local Applebee's today!
